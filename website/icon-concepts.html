Icon concepts

Four directions for the deadlinkprobe icon. Each below is shown at 96px (hero), 32px (favicon-large), and 16px (tab favicon).

A · Inspector

Search · Check · Verify

A magnifying glass with a chain link inside the lens. Universal "inspection" language — anyone reads it as "check links". Survives well at tiny sizes because the silhouette (circle + handle) is unmistakable.

32px
16px (simplified)

✓ Most universally recognised metaphor

✓ Works at every size

— Slightly generic (similar to search apps)

B · Diagnosis

Healthy · Broken · Detected

A chain where one ring is intact (cyan) and the other has a visible crack (red). Shows the app's literal outcome: it finds broken links. Strongest narrative of the three — you don't have to explain it.

32px
16px

✓ Strongest narrative — "finds broken links"

✓ Two-colour story (healthy vs broken)

— Detail loss at 16px; crack reads as noise

C · Probe

Scan · Sweep · Discover

A radar scope sweeping over a chain link, with dots marking discovered links (cyan/green/red = status). Leans into the "probe" in the name. Most distinctive of the three — and animates naturally.

32px
16px (simplified)

✓ Most distinctive, on-brand for "probe"

✓ Animates well at hero size

— Busy; needs heavy simplification at 16px

D · Detective

Inspect · Identify · Resolve

A's magnifying glass meets B's healthy/broken chain — inside the lens. The icon shows both the action (inspecting) and the outcome (one link is broken). Tells the full story of the tool in a single glance.

32px
16px (simplified)

✓ Complete story: action + outcome together

✓ Inherits B's two-colour contrast

— Densest of the four; crack detail lost < 24px

My recommendation

D · Detective is now the strongest pick — it combines A's universally-readable inspection metaphor with B's healthy/broken colour story, so a new viewer sees both what the app does (inspect) and what it finds (a broken link) in one glance. Use B · Diagnosis if you want a purer, cleaner 16px favicon; A · Inspector as the safe fallback; C · Probe for a hero or loading-state animation.
